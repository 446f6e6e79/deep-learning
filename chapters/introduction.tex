\section{Introduction}
In this section we will briefly introduce the basic concepts of deep learning, 
its historical background, and a brief recap of machine learning concepts that are fundamental to understanding deep learning.

\subsection{Basic Definitions}
\paragraph{AI}
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a discipline that has the ambition to create machines that
mimic human intelligence. It encompasses a wide range of techniques and approaches, including rule-based systems, expert systems, and machine learning.
The ultimate goal of AI is to make programs that can learn from their experience as effectively as humans do.

\paragraph{Machine Learning}
Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that uses techniques that enable machines to learn
from experience (i.e. data) to improve their performance on a specific task.

\paragraph{Deep Learning} is an area of Machine Learning that in particular focuses on
\texttt{Artificial Neural Networks} (ANNs). The term "deep" refers to the use of multiple layers
in the network, which allows it to learn complex representations of data, with multiple levels of abstraction.
